Lisa's Arcade Game

Instructions

  1. To start and stop the game, press the spacebar
  2. Use the arrow keys to move your player towards the water (and a little rest)
  3. Watch out for bugs! They'll eat you.
  4. Collect the gems and increase your score. Collect all of the gems and win!
